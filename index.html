Instructions

To play spot the difference, you must do 4 simple steps.

  1. First, hover over any of the difficulty boxes you want to play. It is reccomended to go from easy to hard.

  2. Next, once you choose your box, look at the pre-image for exactly 5 seconds without hovering your mouse over the image.

  3. After seeing your pre-image for 5 seconds, hover your mouse over the image, and you'll see the "after" image.

  4. After finding the after image, try and see what changed!
